Why I Am Starting a Blog

23 Jul 2025

This isn’t my First Attempt At Blogging

Around April 2024, I tried out blogging.

It wasn’t fun - I was trying to build the “perfect audience” and “help people with my course”.

Ye… I see why that didn’t work out.

Why This Blog Is Different

Well, there are a few things I want to share through this blog:

My Projects

A portfolio of sorts. I am 15 (3 months to 16) and think it’s high time I have a portfolio page… atleast a static post on my blog atleast.

It will also be very fun imo to share my though process behind certain projects.

Some projects:

  • AI Based Worksheet Generator For Studying In A More Focused Way (Next JS)
  • Projectile Simulator Using PyGame (mainly to have fun) (there is more but really that’s not the point right now)

Things I Learn

If you didn’t read the home page (lmao) I love coding, tech, physics and maths (just a few things).

Some might say I am a polymath of sorts.

And therefore, I think having a place to share what I learn will be great to help others and to serve as a personal note.

Documentation

Documentation of one’s life is an amazing thing because you can look back and see and cringe and laugh at things.

Ye… I guess that sums it up.

Regularity of This Blog

At the time of writing this, I have no clue how I will manage this.

One thing for sure: I will make it work.

Ending Thoughts

Might make an email list for the ones who are really interested (no spam promise).

Also will just post these blog posts on Medium as well and maybe LinkedIn.

See ya!
